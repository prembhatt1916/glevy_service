The next generation of Recruiters.​

​

Future Recruiter by Glevy is a Rookie Rec-2-Rec training program designed to solve the ever-growing talent shortage in agency recruitment.

With years of experience hiring and training junior candidates, an award-winning service, and using the latest in robust, gamified, assessment technology, Glevy are excited to bring our expertise to the wider recruitment industry.

SOURCE

We are meeting and interviewing prospective rookies daily.

We have vast networks across multiple disciplines and a recognisable brand that attracts candidates

We consider candidates who’ve come from industry, with the drive and motivation to recruit.

We partner with universities and colleges.

We focus on the candidate experience and in turn, earn their trust.

​ASSESS

We use the latest in gamified assessment technology tailored specifically to recruitment, to assess potential rookies.

We interview candidates at length, digging into their drives and motivations.

We align the best-fit rookies to match your business in culture fit, recruitment discipline and career goals.​

TRAIN

We’ve been successfully training rookies for years.

We’ve tailor made an industry specific, interactive Recruitment Foundations Course, to set rookies up to succeed.

Three day immersive training with the Glevy Training Manager. Learn amongst your peers.

Six months of ongoing virtual sessions to solidify learning and deep dive into the nuances of agency recruitment.​​
