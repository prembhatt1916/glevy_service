Payroll Services

Found your perfect person but don’t have Full Time head count?

​

MAYDAY Consulting offers Payroll Services to a number of our customers, having over 100payrolls only contractor’s onsite with our clients at any one time.

​

We offer this unbundled service in a cost-efficient, agile and rapidly scalable fashion to provide greater levels of flexibility and optimise our client's program needs.

​

MAYDAY take care of candidate set-up, compliance, background checks, and the entire payroll function which includes workers compensation, payroll tax, online timesheets and payment and management of Superannuation. 


It’s our duty of care to ensure the safety of these labour-hire staff.  MAYDAY complete regular Workplace Health and Safety checks at client sites, prior to assignment commencement then on an annual basis.


Our highly experienced and responsive locally based Finance team liaise regularly with the Australian Tax Office and the NSW Office of State Revenue on behalf of our clients and offer a direct line of communication with our contingent workforce.


Should your company require the on-boarding of one or multiple contractors quickly, MAYDAY Consulting can manage that process for you end-to-end.  Contact us today for more information on our Payroll Services.
