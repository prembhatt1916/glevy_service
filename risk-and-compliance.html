Risk & Compliance

Contractor resourcing in the Risk and Compliance space is one of MAYDAY Consulting’s core competencies.  


We support Australia’s largest financial institutions and the Big 4 Auditors supplying skilled, in-demand individuals into a highly regulated, risk-averse and changing environments.  Our sourcing remit ranges from Senior Management and SMEs to volume recruits on remediation projects, and with a large database, contractor network and proactive resourcing we can provide quality talent within short turnaround times.


As an example, for one of our major clients, a large global Insurer, we sourced twelve RG146 qualified Case Assessors to complete a nine-month remediation project, all within a three-day turnaround timeframe.


We’re a recruitment partner you can trust….. MAYDAY Consulting have tried and tested recruitment methodologies and compliance checks in place to ensure all our candidates meet our client and industry requirements prior to commencement on any project. 


If your organisation is going through change or review, or if you are seeking your next contract within Risk and Compliance then contact our Managing Director, Lynne Johnston today.
