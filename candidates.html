Candidates

What Can You Expect From Us?

Here at Glevy, we focus on understanding who you are, what you’re looking for, and the kind of work experiences that will allow you to thrive. We know that every candidate we meet is an individual who has a lot to offer- and it’s our job to find out exactly what that is. We know that job seekers want their recruitment experience to be personal, informed, clearly communicated and with fast turn-around times. We also know that everyone wants to have positive working experience, and so we only assist with recruiting for top quality companies who want to do right by their teams. We put candidates forward for jobs they’re excited about, and our candidates can be sure they will be greeted in an interview by a company with a great environment and by future employers who care about their journey. 


Career Consulting

We want you to land your dream job, so expect us to go above and beyond…. Understanding you as a person and uncovering what motivates you will be key to finding the next step in your career, so be prepared to open up and share your aspirations.

From helpful tips on how to perfect your resume, to pre-interview coaching, and providing you with all important feedback, your supportive Glevy consultant will be there to help you every step of the way.  We commit to always representing you professionally and with honesty and integrity.                                                                                                                                            

                                                                                                                                                                                                                   

Top Employers
